KHM ‘23

Kochi Hub Meet 2023 (KHM’23) is the 1-day annual flagship event organized by IEEE Kochi Hub. It serves as a dynamic platform for students within the hub to connect, collaborate, and exchange ideas in the ever-evolving field of technology. KHM'23 stands as a beacon of innovation, collaboration, and knowledge-sharing in the vibrant landscape of technology.
